My journey into the world of technology led me to build a robust foundation in Data Structures and Algorithms. I thrive on navigating intricacies and crafting efficient solutions, drawing inspiration not just from the world of code but also from the dynamic strategies learned on the football field.

Expanding my tech horizons, I delve into the realms of machine learning, exploring artificial intelligence to enhance problem-solving and decision-making.

Whether it's connecting with tech enthusiasts, collaborating on machine learning projects, or sharing experiences about life in India, I'm always eager to explore new possibilities and perspectives. Let's connect and embark on this exciting journey together!
